Hello there! My name is Mike Zhang. I'm a second-year PhD Student at the IT University of Copenhagen under supervision of Prof. Barbara Plank. We are part of the NLPnorth research unit. My main focus is working on automated high-quality Information Extraction from unstructured text with real-life use cases that have societal impact. My other interests include tricks and approaches to get more labeled training data and/or exploit models for tasks with limited data — this includes Active Learning, Weak Supervision, and (more recently) Transfer Learning.
